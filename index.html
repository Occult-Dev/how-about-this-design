Hey... How About This Design?

Brutalism, Minimalism, Vaporwave, Retro, Futuristic... There’s so many design styles out there it can be tough to stay on top of them all!

Here at How About This Design? we help you choose your next great design by showing you real-world examples of various different design styles. Choose a style from the menu below, and watch this site morph right before your eyes!

The content of the site stays mostly the same, it just shifts around, changes palettes, and gets a bit aesthetically funky.
At the bottom of this page are examples from various categories (product design, banners, merch, etc.) that showcase a more specific breakdown of your chosen style.

We hope you find this useful!

About This Design:

Not much to say right now! You are on the homepage for How About This Design?, this homepage is pure HTML, nothing else.
No css, no javascript, just plain and simple. It’s basically just a template, so we don’t have any cool facts or lore for you to read about.

Unless, of course, you wanna read about this site!
Yeah? Really? Ok, cool! (if not, tough shit, I gotta fill this space with something)

Well, let’s see here... This site basically got birthed the moment the creator (that’s me, Spencer!) got an email from Dribbble where they offered a free ‘.design’ domain name thanks to Porkbun (I LOVE porkbun! <3).

This is a good offer! I love buying domain names, it’s like digital land! I had to make use of this coupon, but I didn’t know what kindof domain name I wanted.

Do I build a portfolio site for myself? Nah, I already got my own website I can use for that...
Do I make a design community? Cool, but I feel like plenty already exist and I don’t have any creative ideas to improve upon them...
Do I... Hmm... How about... No... GAH! WHY ARE ALL THE GOOD NAMES ALREADY TAKEN!

And then... BAM! It hit me, how about I make a goose-themed news aggregator! And so I did.

BUT-- Back on track, I still had this whole ‘.design’ thing I needed to address. So, after heavy use of a thesaurus and many minutes of trying out different word combination, I finally landed on a neat idea: A site dedicated to the endless list of different design styles.

And that’s pretty much it. I ended up landing on HowAboutThis.Design as a niffty name, and I got to work building out the design gallery. Thankfully, I announced this idea during Hacktoberfest, so there's plenty of willing contributors to fiddle with various styles!
