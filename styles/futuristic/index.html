About This Design:

The word Futuristic holds the meaning of something that's advanced or something that is way ahead of its time. Meaning that Futuristic design should give the user a taste of how the future could turn out to be.

The future could be many things. It could be a future of hi-tech advancements, human settlements in Mars or any other positive Futuristic outlooks we could think of. Or it could be painted as the apocalypse. Dark, eerie, filled with zombies or AI robots who take over our planet. In the end, when we think of futuristic design, and tech in general, images of the Matrix, Black Panther come to mind.

Incorporating these images into design, most of us tend to think of a design that's clean, simple yet captivating with sleek animations and a high responsiveness.
